Chapter 5. Users and Groups¶

5.1. Users¶

Recognized Users are either Indico locally registered users, NICE users, or users with CERN light-weight accounts.

5.2. Groups¶

Recognized Groups are either Indico locally created groups (only Administrators can create such groups), NICE groups, or e-groups. We strongly recommend the use of SIMBA lists as groups of users in Indico.
